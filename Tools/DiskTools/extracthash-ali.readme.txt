EXTRACTHASH - extracthash-ali.exe 

extracts bootloader hashes

Usage: extracthash-ali.exe <deviceindex or file> 
example extracthash-ali.exe 2


example:
------------------------------------------------
rem extract hashes for disk device 2
rem outputs boot_loader_hash.bin and mbr_hash.bin
extracthash-ali.exe  2
------------------------------------------------
calculates aristocrat specific hashes for mbr (sector 0) and bootloader (sector 1-1006)
