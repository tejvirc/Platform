    Class IdReaderDevice

    The idReader class is used to manage player and employee ID reader (identification reader) devices installed in an EGM. Such devices include, but are not limited to, magnetic card readers, proximity card readers, fingerprint scanners, retina scanners, smart card readers, direct-entry keypads, and Hollerith cards.
