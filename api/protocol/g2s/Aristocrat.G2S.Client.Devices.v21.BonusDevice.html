    Class BonusDevice

    The bonus class is used to manage the process of awarding external bonuses at an EGM. External bonuses encompass a wide variety of game enhancements for the player, such as win multipliers, mystery progressives, and consolation prizes.External bonus awards are paid in addition to the standard paytable awards.
