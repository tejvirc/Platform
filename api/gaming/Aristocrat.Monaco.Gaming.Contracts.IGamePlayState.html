    Idle

    Gets a value indicating whether the game is in play (not in a game-cycle), player input is enabled, player may leave the game and browse the various games available on the EGM. Bonus awards MAY be paid while in the gameIdle state. The player may adjust some game parameters such as lines bet or keno marks, without initiating a game-cycle.
